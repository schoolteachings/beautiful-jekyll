### Problem 1

**Step 1**: Take 20 squard (400) and add it to 40 squared (1600)

{: .box-note}
400 + 1600 = 2000

**Step 2**: Find the square root of 2000

{: .box-note}
√2000 = 44.721


{: .box-warning}
**Final Answer**: x = 44.721
